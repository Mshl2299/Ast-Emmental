HOW TO PLAY

Use the buttons to move the ship.

  • Welcome to AST-Emmental Mining Company™!
    Your task is to mine asteroids and get rich with a highscore!
  • Collect Grey Asteroids // Avoid Red ones
  • Collect the valuable (but heavy) Yellow Asteroids too!
  • Customize MUSIC, SKINS & AUDIO at the top
  • Press the "X" button to RESET
  • Unlock new SKINS with scores! Check scores on the right >
